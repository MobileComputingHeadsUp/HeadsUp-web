How it works

Introduction

HeadsUp will link reality and the digital world in a simple way. Space owners (managers, professors, trainers, etc.) can transform their locales into a vibrant social environment. HeadsUp will enable real people to connect to others nearby. We unlock the potential to create a real time network, allowing those in the network to easily share and connect with others automatically. We will allow users to find others nearby who share common interests in real time. We will also allow users to connect to real objects in spaces in meaningful ways.

If you are a space owner

  • 1. Get a beacon
  • 2. Create a new “space” via our web interface and register that beacon to the space
  • 3. Define a Space Schema for users and devices in the space
  • 4. Register the devices(sensors/ actuators) the space owner wants to make available

If you are a user

  • 1. Enter a beacon zone
  • 2. Provide information requested by the beacon
  • 3. Enter the space dashboard for the space you're currently in
  • 4. Get relevant information about the space!
